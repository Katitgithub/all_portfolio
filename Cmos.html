About the Course

CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) is a key technology in the design and development of integrated circuits. This course covers the fundamental principles of CMOS technology, its uses, and real-world applications.

Applications:

  • Microprocessors and digital logic circuits
  • Memory devices like SRAM and DRAM
  • Analog circuits, including amplifiers and oscillators
  • Image sensors and digital cameras

Course Project

Problem Statement

Design and Implementation of 1-bit Comparator using fully CMOS and Pseudo NMOS logic.
